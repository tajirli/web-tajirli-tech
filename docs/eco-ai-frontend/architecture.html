Layer Responsibilities

  • Presentation: render content and CTAs for public visitors.
  • Language: provide FR, EN, and AR content with RTL behavior.
  • Interaction: validate and submit the contact form.
  • Media: display portfolio and product media assets.
  • Analytics: initialize Vercel tracking on the client side.

Architecture Risks

  • Backend contact API availability affects lead capture.
  • Asset paths must stay stable for portfolio images and logo files.
  • Language switching must remain synchronized with RTL layout.
  • Marketing copy and CTA flow should remain consistent with backend forms.
